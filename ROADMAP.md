# Bridge — Roadmap

## Documents

### [Vision](docs/vision.md)
Why Bridge exists, the problem it's solving, and the design principles behind it.
The silo problem, the difference between describing problems and prescribing actions,
the human-in-the-loop model, and where the project is going long-term.
*Audience: anyone working on or thinking about Bridge.*

### [Technical Roadmap](docs/technical.md)
Feature backlog organized by theme: dispatcher workflow (closing the approve→instruct
loop), algorithm quality, static GTFS integration, multi-agency support, data
persistence, operational context, and infrastructure.
*Audience: developers.*

### [For Agencies](docs/for-agencies.md)
What Bridge does, what data it needs, how recommendations work, how the policy
layer respects operating constraints, and how to get started.
*Audience: transit agencies evaluating Bridge.*
